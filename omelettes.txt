Ingredients:
* 1 egg
* 1 tbsp milk
* 1 slice emmental cheese
* 1/2 slice turkey
* pinch of salt
* pinch of pepper
* 1 tsp butter
* 1 tsp all purpose flour

Steps:
1. beat the egg with the milk,salt and pepper.
2. Add the flour and beat again.
3. Melt the butter in a non-stick sauce pan and then pour the egg mixture.
4. lower the heat and cover the pan with a lid.
5. When it hardens a little,flip the egg and wait for about 30 seconds.
6. Flip again,and add the cheese and turkey on half of the egg.
7. Fold the other half and cover with a lid till the cheese melts.
8. Enjoy!
